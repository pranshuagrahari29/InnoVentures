Current Government Agriculture Schemes in India

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Provides ‚Çπ6,000/year in three installments to eligible farmers to support their income.

https://pmkisan.gov.in

2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Offers low-cost crop insurance to farmers to protect against crop loss due to natural disasters.

https://pmfby.gov.in

3. Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Aims to expand irrigation coverage and improve water use efficiency in agriculture.

https://pmksy.gov.in

4. Soil Health Card Scheme

Distributes soil health cards to farmers with information on soil nutrients and fertilizer use.

https://soilhealth.dac.gov.in

5. Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Offers short-term loans to farmers for agricultural and allied activities at subsidized rates.

KCC Info (PDF)

6. National Agriculture Market (eNAM)

A digital trading platform for agricultural commodities to ensure better price discovery for farmers.

https://enam.gov.in

7. Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Promotes organic farming through group-based training, certification, and marketing support.

https://www.pgsindia-ncof.gov.in

8. Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)

Supports production and post-harvest management of horticulture crops like fruits and vegetables.

https://midh.gov.in

9. Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana ‚Äì RAFTAAR

Focuses on agricultural development, innovation, and entrepreneurship through state-led initiatives.

https://rkvy.nic.in

10. Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Common Farming Tips

  • Test soil regularly to optimize fertilizer usage.
  • Plant crop varieties suitable for your climate.
  • Use crop rotation to maintain soil health.
  • Monitor weather forecasts before irrigation or spraying.
  • Keep farm equipment well-maintained for efficiency.
